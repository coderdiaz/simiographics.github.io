About me

Salvador López Mendoza

I'm a user interface designer focusing principally on user experience for digital products/services as well as web development. I was born in Michoacán, México and after living in various cities around the country, I've since established myself in the beautiful metropolis of México City.

Recently, I've ventured into entrepreneurship with Lingbry, a platform that seeks to make language teaching and learning easier by connecting people with independent teachers around the world. It's been a huge learning experience for me so far, as I have taken on multiple roles such as designing the entire platform, including the user experience as well as the frontend and backend development.

I've also had the opportunity to get involved with the Open Government movement by joining the México Abierto team as a frontend developer, in wich I worked on a couple of projects such as datos.gob.mx, México's official platform for releasing open data to the public and gob.mx/participa, a tool for allowing citizen participation and collaboration for the betterment of public policy.

Throughout my career, I've developed valuable skills in design and coding by forming part of several talented teams, such as:

  • México Abierto, the Mexican Open Government division.

  • Cúrsame, an educational platform for schools.

  • Kueski, México´s leading online lender.

  • Folgom, a business network for selling and buying goods/services.

  • Freshout, a former product development agency.
